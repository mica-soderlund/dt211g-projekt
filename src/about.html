Project Explore Space


About Us

In this project, a website was developed to serve as an information portal to the mysterious galaxy of space, where users can search for facts about space from Wikipedia and view matching images from NASA's extensive image archive. The aim was to create an interactive experience that leverages the latest web technologies to provide users with a unique insight into the wonders of the universe.

The heart of the website is its ability to dynamically fetch and display information and images related to space, using JSON-based web services and APIs from NASA and Wikipedia. This integration has enabled a rich and interactive user experience, where visitors can discover and explore space with facts and images.

We hope you enjoy it!
